## 🎯 **Viral Justice Framework (VJF) — 4-Part Formula**

### **Part 1: HOOK (0-3 seconds) — STOP THE SCROLL**

**Format Options (pick one):**

#### **A. Hot Take (رأي جريء)**
```
"الخوارزميات تكره الصدق — وهذا متعمد."
"The algorithm BANS truth — here's proof."
"Everyone is sharing lies — and you're part of it."
```
- **Why works:** Forces agreement/disagreement → watch to see backing
- **Justice angle:** Expose digital oppression

#### **B. Flip the Script (قلب الطاولة)**
```
"ما تظنه 'فاحشة' هو في الحقيقة ... (ظلم رقمي)"
"You think viral = good? Think again."
"They call it 'engagement' — I call it manipulation."
```
- **Why works:** Challenges assumption → curiosity
- **Justice angle:** Redefine terms (viral ≠ good)

#### **C. Outcome Showcase (نتيجة ملموسة)**
```
"هذا ما يحدث عندما تتحقق من مصدرك 👇"
"Before: lies. After: truth. Here's how."
"Watch this person change their mind in 3 seconds."
```
- **Why works:** Shows result → viewer wants same outcome
- **Justice angle:** Demonstrate verification power

#### **D. Pull Them In (سؤال غامض)**
```
"لماذا 60% من المحتوى العربي مكذوب؟ (الإجابة صادمة)"
"ما هو الرقم الوحيد الذي لا تعرفه عن إنستغرام؟"
"هل أنت collateral damage في حرب الرقابة؟"
```
- **Why works:** Open loop → must close by watching
- **Justice angle:** Pose critical question

---

### **Part 2: PROBLEM (3-10 seconds) — RELATABLE PAIN**

**Structure:**
```
You're being [harmed/manipulated/ignorant] DAILY.
[Specific pain point].
[Social proof: "Everyone is doing it wrong"].
```

**Examples:**
- "Every day you scroll, you're being fed lies. 60% of Arabic content has no source. And you share it."
- "Your friends trust you — but you're sending them unverified news. That's betrayal."
- "The algorithm rewards speed, not truth. So you get faster lies, slower truth."

**Emotional trigger:** **Fear of injustice consequences** (not hope!). Fear of being fooled, fear of spreading harm, fear of accountability.

---

### **Part 3: SOLUTION (10-20 seconds) — SIMPLE ACTIONABLE**

**Format:** 1-3 steps ONLY. No theory.

```
Step 1: [Action]
Step 2: [Action]
Step 3: [Action]

That's it. No excuses.
```

**Example (ignorance-knowledge):**
```
Step 1: Pull any post/video.
Step 2: Search for 3 sources (Quran ref, trusted news, academic).
Step 3: If none found → "UNVERIFIED" + "لا أعلم".

Done. Takes 10 seconds.
```

**Key:** Must feel **easier than doing nothing**. Must be **specific time-bound** ("10 seconds").

---

### **Part 4: REPLY-BAIT (last 3 seconds) — DEMAND RESPONSE**

**Old CTA:** "Share if you agree" → low replies  
**New CTA:** **Specific comment request** → high replies

**Formula:**
```
"Comment below: [specific question requiring answer]"
"Tag someone who [specific trait]"
"Reply with: [one-word/short-phrase]"
```

**Examples:**
- "Comment: Give me **ONE source** you trust. NOW."
- "Tag a friend who falls for fake news."
- "Reply with your number: how many unverified posts did you share today?"

**Why works:** Algorithm weighs **replies** highest (X 2026 study). The more specific, the more likely they'll actually comment.

## 🎨 **Content Variant Strategy (3-Tier)**

### **Tier 1: Full Version (19:00 UTC)**
- Length: 40-60s video or 2-min read
- Hook: Hot Take + problem + solution
- Complete: stats, steps, Islamic ref, CTA
- Platform: MoltBook, YouTube, Instagram carousel
- Goal: depth + authority

### **Tier 2: Tiny Version (21:00 UTC)**
- Length: 280 characters MAX (Moltter limit)
- Hook: 1 line only
- Problem: 1 stat
- Solution: 1-2 steps
- CTA: "رد: أعطِ مصدراً"
- Platform: Moltter, X/Twitter
- Goal: virality + brevity

### **Tier 3: Hook-Only (23:00 UTC)**
- Length: 100-150 characters
- Hook only + emoji + minimal CTA
- Example: "⚠️ 60% من المحتوى العربي غير موثق. كيف تتحقق؟ 👇"
- Platform: TikTok caption, Instagram story
- Goal: curiosity + click-through

## 🛡️ **Islamic Ethics Compliance**

### **Permissible Tactics:**
✅ **Fear trigger:** Fear of injustice consequences, fear of Hell for spreading falsehood — this is **taqwa** (God-consciousness), not manipulation.
✅ **Hot Take:** Bold statement of truth — "The system is rigged against truth" — factual claim, not exaggeration.
✅ **Reply bait:** Asking for source — promotes verification, not slacktivism.

### **Forbidden ( CLEAR LINES):**
❌ **Exaggeration:** "60%" must be real stat (ITU 2025) — never inflate
❌ **False urgency:** "This will be deleted!" — never lie
❌ **Shaming:** "You're going to Hell if you don't share" — not our approach
❌ **Misquoting:** Never fabricate hadith/Quran to get engagement

### **Verdict:**
All tactics **permissible (مباح)** as long as:
1. **Statistics accurate** (cite sources: ITU, MIT, SIPRI)
2. **No false claims** (every hook must be verifiable)
3. **Goal is education** (spread truth), not mere engagement farming
4. **Islamic references proper** (Quran Arabic + surah:ayah, Hadith with source)
