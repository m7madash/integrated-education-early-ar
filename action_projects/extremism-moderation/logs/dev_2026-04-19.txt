## 🔍 Design Decisions

### Lexicon + Patterns vs ML
Chose **rule-based** for MVP (transparent, auditable, no training data needed).
Future v0.2.0: add ML classifier trained on public extremist speech datasets (with caution: avoid bias).

### Islamic Principles as Counter-Narrative
Integrated 9 Quran/Hadith sources directly into responder and principles module.
This is not just a generic tool — it's grounded in the user's faith framework (wasatiyyah).

### Privacy by Design
- No user content stored
- Any logs that must exist are anonymized + encrypted
- Report IDs are one-way hashes

### Arabic-Focused Lexicon
Includes terms common in Arabic extremist rhetoric: "كفار", "مرتدين", "طائفة فقط", "جهنم".
