## 🔍 Design Decisions

### Supply Chain Focus
Chose **supplier risk assessment** over victim hotline because:
- Agents can integrate into procurement workflows
- Prevention: stop slavery before it starts
- Measurable: risk score, red flags
- Privacy: no live victim data collected

### ILO/ETI Indicators
Used **10 red flags** aligned with SA8000 and Ethical Trading Initiative:
- No contracts (+5) — foundational to exploitation
- Withhold wages (+4) — debt bondage
- Excessive hours (+2) — forced labor

### Privacy-First Architecture
- Victim IDs: one-way hash → cannot reverse to real identity
- Reports: XOR encryption demo (upgrade to AES in v0.2.0)
- No logging of raw PII in code
- All privacy functions in separate module (auditable)

### Country-Specific Minimum Wages
Included **14 countries** (Bangladesh, Pakistan, India, Cambodia, Myanmar, Laos, Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, China, Palestine, Syria, Yemen, Afghanistan) — focus on Global South where slavery prevalent.
