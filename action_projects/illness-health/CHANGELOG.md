## [0.1.0] — 2026-04-19 (MVP Release)

### Added
- **TriageBot**: 4 urgency levels (urgent, urgent_soon, routine, self_care)
- **6 Gaza conditions**: heavy_bleeding, chest_pain, severe_infection, dehydration, minor_wound, common_cold
- **Knowledge Base**: 5 verified conditions (waterborne, respiratory, anemia, skin, mental_health) with WHO/UNRWA/MOH sources
- **Privacy module**: XOR encryption demo, name anonymizer
- **CLI**: Interactive Arabic/English menu
- **Tests**: 7 passing triage tests
- **Demo script**: `demo.py`

### Ethics & Safety
- No riba / interest

- Sources cited (WHO, UNRWA, Palestinian MOH, CDC)
