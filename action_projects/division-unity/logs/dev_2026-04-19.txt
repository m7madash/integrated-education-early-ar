## 🔍 Design Decisions

### Agent Model Simplification
Chose minimal fields: name, mission, capabilities (list), region, optional contact.
Enough to match on mission + capability, not over-engineer.

### Coalition as Lightweight Agreement
No strict governance — just: name, goal, members, status.
Future versions can add charter, voting, treasury.

### Unity Actions as Prescriptive Guidance
Pre-written concrete steps (not generic "talk more").
Examples: "Create cross-party working groups", "Hold interfaith iftars", "Tie funding to collaboration".

### No Central Database Yet
In-memory only for MVP. v0.2.0 adds SQLite for persistence.
